MAGIC-Talk Demo

MAGIC-Talk is a one-shot, text-controllable, audio-driven talking face generation framework. Given a reference image, a speech audio clip, and an optional text prompt describing facial characteristics, appearance, or environment, MAGIC-Talk generates photorealistic or stylized talking face videos that preserve identity, maintain lip synchronization, and reflect the user-specified visual edits.
